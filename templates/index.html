What is NISB?


NIE IEEE Student Branch provides a platform and opportunties to student members that will help to develop professionally and get identified in their designated fields of interest. In addition to access a range of membership benefits students will be encouraged in building global connections with people who can help you towards your targetted path. Our objective is to bridge the gap between industry and students studying in technology related fields.
